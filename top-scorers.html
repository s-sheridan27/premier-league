TOP SCORERS OF THE DECADE

The following shows us a list of the golden boot winners of the decade. Three players won the golden boot twice across the decade. The highest single season tally was Mo Salah's return of 32 goals for Liverpool in the 2017/18 season. Sergio Aguero was the highest scorer across the decade with 174 goals, 38 more than Harry Kane in 2nd place.
